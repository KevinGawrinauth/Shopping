Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is GreenGrocer?

GreenGrocer is an online marketplace for fresh produce and grocery items delivered to your door.

How do I place an order?

Select items from our categories, add them to your cart, and checkout using your preferred payment method.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and more.

Can I track my order?

Yes, order tracking is available once your order has been shipped.
